    How Jadoo Works

    Connecting brands with creators for authentic, affordable, and impactful marketing.

    Create a Campaign

    Tell us your goal whether it’s product awareness, website traffic, or app installs.

    Then choose how you want to work with creators:

    • Grab & Go: Creators claim and run your campaign instantly.
    • Spotlight: You pick from handpicked creator pitches.
    Set a Budget

    Every campaign starts with a minimum base budget this ensures creators are fairly compensated and your campaign meets our quality standards.

    Show more

    Beyond the base, your total budget depends on several key choices:

    • Creator Tier – Choose between nano, micro, or macro creators. Higher tiers may require a larger investment.
    • Campaign Type – Awareness-based campaigns typically differ in pricing compared to performance-driven ones (e.g., app installs or link clicks).
    • Budget Distribution – You can choose to work with one creator or spread your budget across multiple creators for wider reach and diversity in content.

    No matter your budget as long as it meets our minimum threshold we’ll always match you with the right creators to make your campaign successful. You’re in control, and we’ll handle the heavy lifting.
